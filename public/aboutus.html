About Us

Founded by Sami, Salavat and Luca, Plantable was created in 2025 as a revolutionary product for plant watering.

In the past, you would have to set up tedious sprinkler system just to water your plants! But, with our new Planter Pro, simply enter the name of your plant into your mobile app, and we'll do the rest.

And, with our database of over 2000 plants and their watering information, you can trust that we won't under or overwater your plant.
